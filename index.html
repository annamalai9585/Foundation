About

  • The We Can Voice for Women Foundationis a grassroots non-governmental organization (NGO) committed to fostering gender equity and empowering women across India. Established in 2019, the Foundation was born out of a deep-seated belief that women’s empowerment is not just a social imperative but a national priority essential for inclusive and sustainable development.
  • We believe that true empowerment is multi-dimensional and must address the foundational pillars of education, healthcare, economic independence, legal protection, and social inclusion. Through our work, we aim to dismantle societal barriers, advocate for women's rights, and build an ecosystem where women can thrive with dignity and autonomy.

Fund




A women's foundation collection fund, also known as a women's fund or women's grant, is a charitable organization dedicated to supporting women's rights and empowerment. These funds typically raise money from individual donors, foundations, and other sources to provide grants and resources to women-led organizations and grassroots movements working to advance gender equality. Their goal is to amplify the work of women's rights activists and organizations, ultimately achieving global gender equality.


Global Fund for Women

This is a large international grantmaking foundation that supports organizations working on women's rights worldwide.


L'Oreal Fund for Women

This fund supports grassroots organizations working on issues like sexual violence and access to education.


International Network of Women's Funds

This network connects women's funds around the world, providing resources, advice, and mentoring.


Mama Cash

One of the oldest international women's funds, focused on supporting feminist collective action.


The Cherie Blair Foundation for Women:

This foundation provides grants and support for women entrepreneurs in developing countries.
